I create smooth user experiences and appealing user interfaces

Hi, I’m Kaleb a UX/UI Designer currently working at Shopper POS a Mexican Startup.

I use my days developing ideas that support and meet the business objectives of the company I work for, without losing track of the needs and goals of our users. I consider this balance crucial to bring value and change things for the better in our society.

Visually speaking I like to strike the right balance between familiarity and eye catching, always being mindful of the little details.

Besides UX and UI I also have extensive experience with front end technologies (HTML, CSS, JS) and online marketing (SEO, SEM, Analytics) I think this experience makes me a well rounded team member.

On my freetime I like to cook new recipes, watch a movie or read a book.
