UX/UI Designer

I create smooth user experiences and appealing user interfaces

Hi, I’m Kaleb a UX/UI Designer from Mexico City.

I work developing ideas that meet the business objectives of the company I work for, without losing track of the needs of our users. I consider this balance crucial to bring value and be different.

Visually speaking I like to strike the right balance between familiarity and eye-catching, always being mindful of the little details.

Besides my knowledge of UX and UI, I also have extensive experience with front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JS) and online marketing (SEO, SEM, Analytics) I think this experience makes me a well-rounded team member.

On my free time, you can find me cooking new recipes, watching a movie or reading a book.
