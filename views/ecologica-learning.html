Brief introduction

Ecologica Learning is a local HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment) training company. The company goal is to offer training and certifications to the MÃ©xico Oil and Gas Industry employees in a way that is convenient for them.

The problem

The existing local services did not have an extensive selection of courses and are not flexible enough to be able to work with the Oil and Gas Industry employees best interest. Currently, there is a need to periodically renew the HSE Certifications as they are mandatory to work in that industry.

Main roles

There are two main roles with this project, Ecologica Learning company who gives the training and the employees of the Oil and Gas industry who take the training.

Context

To take the training the employees have to find a certificated training company that teaches about a particular subject. Then the employee has to go to the training company facility to take the course.

Constraints and scope

HSE courses are practical, their certification requires by law to be face-to-face so it's not possible to offer online courses. Furthermore, information like the course price will not be available in the web app as it changes according to the employees' needs. Also, the company doesn't have a logo or branding yet so that will have to be provided.

The solution

The solution proposed for a first stage is a responsive website that let the employee find the course he is looking for and also contact Ecologica Learning so a course can be scheduled. In a future stage is considered also to show the availability in a calendar for the training.

Conclusion

The web app for Ecologica Learning improves the communication flow between the service company and the oil and gas industry employees that want to be trained. As this web app gets further quantitative and qualitative statistics from their users, it can be improved with more functionality that aligns with both Ecologica Learning and their clients' needs.
